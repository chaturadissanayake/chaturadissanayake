Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This is a personal portfolio site. I want to be straightforward about what happens when you visit it: no corporate boilerplate, just a plain account of what I collect and why.

What I collect

Usage data & Analytics

When you visit the site, basic technical information is collected automatically via Google Analytics. This includes your IP address, browser type, location data, which pages you visited, and how long you spent on them. This helps me understand how the site is being used so I can improve the portfolio.

If you're on a mobile device, this may also include your device type, operating system, and mobile browser.

Cookies

This site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies provided by Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns. A cookie is a small file placed on your device. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or use a tracking blocker, and the site will still function perfectly.

Contact form

If you choose to fill in the contact form, I will receive your name, email address, organisation (if you include it), and your message. I use this information solely to reply to your inquiry. It is processed securely through Formspree, a third-party form service, and sent directly to my inbox.

How I use your data

The only data I have is what you voluntarily send me through the contact form. I use it strictly to respond to you. I do not sell data, share it with advertisers, or add you to any marketing lists.

How long I keep it

I only keep personal data for as long as it's needed for the purpose it was collected, or as long as required to meet any legal obligations. Contact form submissions are kept only as long as the conversation is relevant.

Where it's processed

Your data may be processed outside Sri Lanka, depending on where the third-party services I use are based. By using this site, you're agreeing to that. I only use services I trust to handle data responsibly.

Links to other sites

The site links out to other places: Medium, LinkedIn, GitHub, UN publications, and project links. Once you leave this site, I have no control over what those sites collect. It's worth checking their privacy policies if you're concerned.

Changes

If this policy changes, I'll update the date at the top. It's worth checking back occasionally if this matters to you.

Questions

If you have questions about any of this, email me directly at consultchatura@gmail.com.
