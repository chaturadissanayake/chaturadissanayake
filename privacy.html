Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This is a personal portfolio site. I want to be straightforward about what happens when you visit it: no corporate boilerplate, just a plain account of what I collect and why.

What I collect

Usage data

When you visit the site, basic technical information is collected automatically: things like your IP address, browser type, which pages you visited, and how long you spent on them. This happens through standard web infrastructure and is used only to understand how the site is being used so I can improve it.

If you're on a mobile device, this may also include your device type, mobile operating system, and mobile browser type.

Contact form

If you fill in the contact form, I'll receive your name, email address, organisation (if you include it), and your message. I use that information to reply to you; nothing else. It's processed through Formspree, a third-party form service.

Cookies

The site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how it's being used. A cookie is a small file placed on your device by the site. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though some parts of the site may work less well if you do. The site may also use web beacons: small files that help count page visits and email opens.

How I use it

The information collected is used to keep the site running, understand how people are using it, and make improvements over time. I don't sell data, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything unrelated to running this site.

How long I keep it

I only keep personal data for as long as it's needed for the purpose it was collected, or as long as required to meet any legal obligations. Contact form submissions are kept only as long as the conversation is relevant.

Where it's processed

Your data may be processed outside Sri Lanka, depending on where the third-party services I use are based. By using this site, you're agreeing to that. I only use services I trust to handle data responsibly.

Links to other sites

The site links out to other places: Medium, LinkedIn, GitHub, UN publications, and project links. Once you leave this site, I have no control over what those sites collect. It's worth checking their privacy policies if you're concerned.

Changes

If this policy changes, I'll update the date at the top. It's worth checking back occasionally if this matters to you.

Questions
