Ceylon Harvest Co.

A 3,000-year-old tradition, presented with modern premium confidence.

Consumer Brands Brand Identity
“We speak with quiet confidence. Our language is precise, factual, and grounded in expertise; never ornamental, never borrowed from the tourism playbook.”

Ceylon Harvest Co. is a premium Sri Lankan export brand. The objective is to present Ceylon cinnamon, black pepper, traditional herbs, and natural Sri Lankan products, traceable to source, verified for quality, built for global markets.

The target audience consists of Western consumers and trade buyers who care about origin, quality, and authenticity. They distrust generic. They pay for real. Therefore, the brand must feel ancient and trustworthy. We speak with quiet confidence. Our language is precise, factual, and grounded in expertise, never ornamental, never borrowed from the tourism playbook.

The initial visual exploration utilized solar motifs to signify growth and the tropical climate of origin. While visually striking, the sun icon inadvertently edged toward aesthetics commonly associated with the tourism sector. To truly capture a quiet, unwavering heritage authority, the visual identity required a more grounded, source-driven approach.

The botanical seal communicates transparency, traceability, and absolute authenticity. Every element within the brand seal is a real product, traceable to a real place of origin. There is no decorative filler—the complexity of the mark is earned by the breadth of what Sri Lankan soil genuinely produces.

Our typographic system balances the weight of a 3,000-year-old tradition with the functional, minimalist requirements of modern global commerce. Baskerville Old Face serves as our primary typeface, grounding the brand in history. This is paired with Poppins, a geometric sans-serif that brings modern utility.
