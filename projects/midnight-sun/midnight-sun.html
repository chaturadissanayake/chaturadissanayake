Midnight Sun – Part I

A 1950s sci-fi pin-up fantasy, reimagined as album cover art for an independent band's debut release.

Entertainment Packaging
“Retro pool party… in outer space.” That was the entire brief. Everything else was mine to build.
Design rationale — Delivery 1 (PDF)
Design rationale — Delivery 2 (PDF)

Midnight Sun – Part I is a custom album cover for an independent band's streaming release, blending 1950s sci-fi with pin-up art set at a retro pool party in outer space. The scene features UFOs, pyramids, vintage swimsuits, and cosmic backdrops.

The deliverables included a 4000×4000px main cover, a layered high-resolution source file for vinyl and print, plus reframed crops for YouTube, Spotify Canvas, and social media, all from a single composition.

The first draft depicted a poolside scene with sunbathers in bubble helmets, a UFO beaming up a swimmer, distant pyramids, and a live band. Feedback led to adjustments: the band’s instrumentation was revised, some human figures were replaced with aliens, and extraneous elements were removed for clarity.

The final version introduced a bold, vintage sci-fi typography for the title, enhancing the artwork's 1950s B-movie feel. The finished piece was delivered as a layered PSD with flattened exports for streaming and print use.
